# Final Price Recommendation

## Recommended Launch Price

```text
BountyLens Personal - $19
```

## Why $19

- New product launches should reduce purchase friction.
- BountyLens includes a Chrome extension, Python CLI, Codex prompt pack, landing page, examples, screenshots, and sales materials.
- The first goal is to get feedback from the first 3 to 5 users.
- A low paid price signals that this is a complete workflow, not a loose script.
- The product does not promise guaranteed bounty earnings.

## Future Price Movement

After early feedback and better proof, raise the Personal price to:

- $29 after initial buyer feedback and product polish.
- $39 after screenshots, demo video, and rules improve.

## Future Tiers

Do not launch Pro and Team immediately. Keep them as future tiers:

- Pro: $49
- Team/Internal: $149

Launch with one simple $19 product first. Add Pro or Team only after buyers ask for commercial use, team sharing, more prompts, or internal workflow docs.

## Messaging Boundary

Do not promise:

- Guaranteed bounty earnings.
- Automatic bounty claims.
- Automatic PR success.
- Payment, payout, KYC, tax, wallet, or withdrawal handling.

Position BountyLens around:

- Saving screening time.
- Avoiding obviously bad bounty candidates.
- Reducing risky or wrong PR attempts.
- Keeping the workflow local-first and safety-first.
