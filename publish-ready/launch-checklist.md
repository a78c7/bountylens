# Manual Launch Checklist

This checklist is for the user to perform manually. Do not automate platform login, product creation, uploads, payout setup, KYC, tax, or publication.

## Gumroad

1. Log in to Gumroad.
2. Create product.
3. Choose digital product.
4. Product name: BountyLens.
5. Price: $19.
6. Upload `bountylens-0.1.0.zip`.
7. Upload screenshots in the order listed in `image-upload-order.md`.
8. Paste description from `gumroad-upload-fields.md`.
9. Paste refund policy from `gumroad-upload-fields.md` or `refund-policy.md`.
10. Add privacy note from `privacy-policy.md`.
11. Manually complete payout / tax / KYC requirements.
12. Preview product.
13. Publish.

## Lemon Squeezy

1. Log in to Lemon Squeezy.
2. Create product.
3. Create variant: Personal.
4. Price: $19.
5. Upload ZIP: `bountylens-0.1.0.zip`.
6. Upload screenshots in the order listed in `image-upload-order.md`.
7. Paste listing text from `lemon-squeezy-upload-fields.md`.
8. Keep license keys off for v0.1.
9. Manually complete payout / tax / KYC requirements.
10. Preview.
11. Publish.

## Final Pre-Publish Check

- Confirm the ZIP downloads and opens locally.
- Confirm the first screenshot is `screenshots/png/01-extension-panel.png`.
- Confirm the listing says the toolkit does not guarantee bounty earnings.
- Confirm the listing says BountyLens does not handle payment, KYC, payout, withdrawal, wallet, or tax workflows.
- Confirm support contact placeholder is replaced before publishing.
