# Refund Policy

Template effective date: June 7, 2026

BountyLens is a downloadable digital product. This refund policy is a template and should be adapted before publishing.

## Refund Window

Customers may request a refund within 7 days of purchase.

## Refunds May Be Approved When

- The downloaded product cannot be opened.
- Required files are missing from the package.
- The product materially differs from the listing description.

## Refunds May Be Declined When

- The customer expected guaranteed bounty earnings.
- The product was used for an unsupported custom workflow.
- The customer attempted to use it for KYC, payment, withdrawal, crypto, security, auth, or production database work.
- The issue is caused by third-party platform changes outside the product scope.

## No Earnings Guarantee

BountyLens does not guarantee bounty eligibility, acceptance, merge status, review speed, payout, or revenue.

## How To Request A Refund

Customers should reply through the purchase platform with:

- Order email.
- Purchase date.
- A short description of the problem.
- Screenshots or logs if relevant.

## Template Notice

This policy is a starting point. Review and adapt it for your jurisdiction, sales platform, and customer support process.
