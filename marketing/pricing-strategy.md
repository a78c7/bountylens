# Pricing Strategy

## Launch Pricing

- Personal: $19
- Pro: $49
- Team/Internal: $149

## Personal - $19

Best for one developer evaluating public GitHub bounty issues.

Includes:

- Chrome extension
- Python CLI
- Prompt pack
- Landing/demo examples
- Personal use

## Pro - $49

Best for a solo developer using bounty screening as part of a commercial workflow.

Includes:

- Everything in Personal
- Commercial use for solo developer
- Future minor updates
- Bonus prompt pack

## Team/Internal - $149

Best for a small team that wants a shared triage workflow.

Includes:

- Internal team use up to 5 seats
- Shared workflow docs
- Team safety checklist

## Recommended First Launch

Start with one $19 product.

Reasons:

- Lower purchase friction.
- Simpler product page.
- Easier customer support.
- Faster validation of whether buyers care about the workflow.

Use the phrase:

```text
Launch price: $19
```

After customer feedback, split into Pro and Team variants if users ask for team permissions, commercial usage clarity, or more advanced prompt packs.

## Why Not Free

Free makes the product look like a loose script instead of a packaged workflow. A $19 price still keeps the product accessible while signaling that buyers get a complete extension, CLI, prompt pack, docs, examples, and launch-ready materials.

## Why Not High-Ticket Yet

BountyLens is a v0.1.0 digital toolkit. It does not include hosted automation, managed support, platform integrations, analytics, or guaranteed outcomes. High-ticket pricing would need stronger proof, screenshots, testimonials, workflow videos, and support terms.

## When To Raise Price

Raise the price after:

- 5 to 10 real buyers.
- Clear feedback that the tool saved screening time.
- Real screenshots and demo video are added.
- Rules are improved from buyer feedback.
- Team usage or commercial usage requests appear.

## Messaging Boundaries

Do not promise:

- Guaranteed bounty earnings.
- Automatic bounty claims.
- Automatic PR wins.
- Payment, payout, KYC, wallet, or withdrawal support.

Emphasize:

- Saved screening time.
- Fewer obviously bad candidates.
- Lower risk of wrong PR attempts.
- Local-first safety boundaries.
